# Discord Alert Bot

The bot posts high-scoring deals to a Discord channel.

## Trigger condition

deal_score > 30

## Alert format

🔥 GPU DEAL ALERT

GPU: RTX 3080
Price: $330
Market: $430
Discount: 23%

Deal Score: 38

Link:
https://ebay.com/xxxxx

## Rate limiting

Avoid duplicate alerts.

Only alert once per listing_id.
